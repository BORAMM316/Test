LOCATION AND
HOW TO GET THERE

Bangkok is the capital of Thailand and one of the largest cities in Asia.

By plane: Bangkok has two main airports, Sucarnavhumi Airport and Don Muang Airport.
Suvarnabhumi Airport is used by many international airlines, while Don Muang is home to Nok Air, Orient Thai and Air Asia, mostly serving domestic routes, and short international flights.

Both airports anr around 30 KM from the centre of Bangkok, with the taxi journey being around 30 minutes to 1 hour, heavily depending on traffic, and costing around 300 to 500 THB.
